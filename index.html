Numerical Methods are fun!

This repository contains the source code and homework for many examples of numerical methods and exercises for Physics and Astrophysics.

  • Integration and Differentiation: numerical derivatives, error estimates, Simpson's rule. Here is the whitepaper for the theory.

  • Ordinary Differential Equations: Eulder and Euler-Cromer methods, Verlet method. Here is the whitepaper for the theory.

  • Linear Algebra and FFTs: time series for the damped driven pendulum, matrix inverse, conditional number. Here is the whitepaper for the theory.

  • Advection and Hyperbolic Partial Differential Equations: implicit advection, Lax Wendroff, non-linear hyperbolic equations.

  • Elliptic Partial Differential Equations.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics: Rayleigh-Taylor Instabilities. Here is the whitepaper for the theory.
